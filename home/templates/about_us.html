About Us: FoodBridge

Our Mission

At FoodBridge, our mission is to ensure that surplus food from hotels and restaurants reaches those who need it most. We strive to reduce food wastage and alleviate hunger in India by partnering with hospitality establishments across the country.

Each day, numerous hotels and restaurants prepare more food than is consumed. This surplus, which would otherwise go to waste, holds the potential to feed individuals and families facing food insecurity. By collaborating with our generous partners, we collect this excess food and distribute it to various communities in need.

India, despite being a top food producer, faces significant challenges related to food wastage and hunger. Millions of Indians suffer from malnutrition and food insecurity, especially in rural areas and urban slums. By redirecting surplus food from our partners, we aim to make a direct impact on reducing hunger and promoting food security.

Join us in our commitment to ensuring that no one in India goes to bed hungry. Together, we can make a meaningful difference in the lives of those who need it most.
