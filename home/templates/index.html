Our Activities

At FoodBridge, we are committed to several activities aimed at reducing food wastage and alleviating hunger in India:

  • Food Collection: Partnering with hotels and restaurants to collect surplus food daily.
  • Food Distribution: Distributing collected food to various communities in need.
  • Community Outreach: Conducting awareness campaigns and engaging with local communities.
  • Volunteer Programs: Organizing volunteer activities to support our mission.
  • Educational Workshops: Hosting workshops on food wastage and sustainable practices.
