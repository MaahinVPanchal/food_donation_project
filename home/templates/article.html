Emily Rehberger

“Working at Food Shift was such a valuable experience for me in so many different ways! Everything that Dana has accomplished in such a short amount of time is so inspiring, and working with a small, but mighty and dedicated team was incredible. Dana has a great depth of knowledge about our food system, and the passion that she brings to her work is unparalleled. Food Shift’s efforts to end food waste are changing communities, businesses, the environment, our entire food system, and of course they’ve changed my own personal behaviors as well! For me, working with Food Shift has meant great conversations, learning valuable office and communication skills, and new friendships. I am excited to see where they go next!”

Alys Spillman, Food Shift Grant Assistant

“Working with Food Shift has been an invaluable springboard to a career in grant writing for nonprofits. More than any certification course, this taught me the day to day requirements and processes used to do grant writing from initial research to letters of thanks. The experience was instrumental in securing the contract I now hold as a grant writer. The team at Food Shift has managed to be present and work very collaboratively with me in spite of the fact that I live on the opposite coast. They are deeply passionate about the interconnected issues of food waste and hunger, and I would absolutely recommend volunteering with this incredible organization!”
